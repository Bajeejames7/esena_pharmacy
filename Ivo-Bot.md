## ❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### General Questions

**Q: Is Esena Pharmacy licensed?**
A: Yes, we are fully licensed by the Pharmacy & Poisons Board (PPB) with License No: PPB/PH/01/2024/001.

**Q: Do you deliver nationwide?**
A: Yes, we deliver to all towns and cities across Kenya. Same-day delivery in Nairobi, 1-3 days for other areas.

**Q: What are your business hours?**
A: Monday-Friday: 8AM-8PM, Saturday: 9AM-6PM, Sunday: 10AM-4PM. Online orders accepted 24/7.

**Q: How can I contact you?**
A: Phone: 0768103599, Email: esenapharmacy@gmail.com, or visit our Ruaraka branch.

### Product & Ordering

**Q: How do I search for products?**
A: Use the search bar on the products page, or browse by categories like Pain Relief, Supplements, etc.

**Q: Can I upload my prescription online?**
A: Yes, use our Upload Prescription page. Our pharmacist will review it within 2 hours and provide a quote.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept Mpesa, Visa, and Mastercard. All payments are secure and encrypted.

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A: Use the Track Order page with your order number, or check your email for tracking information.

### Health & Safety

**Q: Are your medications genuine?**
A: Yes, we only stock authentic pharmaceuticals from trusted manufacturers with proper storage and handling.

**Q: Can I get health advice?**
A: Yes, book a pharmacist consultation for professional medication guidance and health advice.

**Q: Do you offer vaccinations?**
A: Yes, we provide flu shots, travel vaccines, and other immunizations. Book an appointment online.

**Q: Is my prescription information confidential?**
A: Absolutely. We maintain strict confidentiality and comply with Kenya's health data protection regulations.

### Technical Support

**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Currently, you can shop as a guest. Account creation features are being developed.

**Q: The website isn't loading properly. What should I do?**
A: Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or contact our support team.

**Q: Can I change my delivery address?**
A: Contact us immediately at 0768103599 if you need to change your delivery address before dispatch.
